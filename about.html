About Focus Timer

Focus Timer is a web application created to help individuals enhance their productivity and concentration. In a world full of countless distractions, we believe in the need for a tool that helps users dive deeper into their goals.

This timer is based on the famous time management method, the 'Pomodoro Technique.' By alternating between short, focused work sessions and regular breaks, it helps reduce mental fatigue and maintain high efficiency over long periods. Focus Timer guides you through this process in an intuitive and easy-to-follow way.

Key Features

  • Simple Timer Setup: A default setting of 25 minutes for work and 5 minutes for breaks is provided, with the ability to adjust the times as needed.
  • Intuitive Interface: We offer a clean and clear design that anyone can use with ease.
  • Web-Based Accessibility: You can use it immediately on any device with a web browser, without any installation required.
  • Notification Function: Clear notifications guide you to your next action at the end of each session.

Our Vision

We believe that technology should play a positive role in maximizing human potential. Starting with Focus Timer, we will continue to develop a variety of productivity tools to support user growth.
