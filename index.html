Maximum Focus with the Pomodoro Timer

Focus Timer is designed to maximize your work and study efficiency.
Repeat short bursts of focus and rest with the Pomodoro Technique to achieve your best performance without burnout.

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. The core of the technique is to use a timer to work in 25-minute intervals, separated by short breaks. This method has several benefits:

  • Improved Focus: By concentrating on a single task for a short period, you block out distractions.
  • Prevents Burnout: Regular breaks give your brain time to recharge, reducing fatigue.
  • Better Time Management: Planning and executing tasks in time-based units helps you use your time more effectively.

How to Use Focus Timer

  1. Click the Start Button: Press 'Start' to begin the 25-minute timer.
  2. Focus and Rest: Concentrate on your work according to the timer, and take a short break when it's time. An alarm will sound when the session ends.
  3. Reset: Pressing 'Reset' will stop the current session and return the timer to the default work time.
