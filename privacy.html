Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 29, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Focus Timer. We take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy describes what information is collected, how it is used, and with whom it is shared when you use our web application.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) from our users. All functionalities of the application operate entirely within your web browser, and we do not operate a backend server to store user data.

However, we may collect information through third-party services as described below:

  • Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our site. This service collects anonymous data such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and browser type, which helps us improve the user experience. You can find more details in the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Microsoft Clarity: We use Microsoft Clarity to capture how you use and interact with our website through behavioral metrics, heatmaps, and session replay to improve and market our products/services. Website usage data is captured using first and third-party cookies and other tracking technologies to determine the popularity of products/services and online activity. For more information about how Microsoft collects and uses your data, visit the Microsoft Privacy Statement.

3. Use of Information

Anonymous data collected through third-party services is used solely for the purposes of website improvement and user behavior analysis. We do not use this information for any other purpose.

4. Data Storage and Security

The only data stored on your device is your theme setting (light/dark mode), which is saved in your browser's `localStorage`. This information is stored locally on your computer and is not transmitted to us.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage you to be aware when you leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us
